Personal growth

Upon reflection, the process of finally learning to code taught me one important thing: I was often my own biggest blocker. Many of the excuses I had made were rarely based in reality, but instead justification not to start.
When I came across Start2impact I instantly fancied the idea of “learning by doing”. Moreover, as I was learning HTML and CSS I realized that these two powerful languages do not just provide the foundation of a website but they also allow you to bring out your personality on your own website and enhance your creativity in order to stand out and create the unexpected.
As a full stack development student starting from square one, my intention is to learn valuable skills that I can build upon for years to come.
